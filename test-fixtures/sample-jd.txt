SENIOR SOFTWARE ENGINEER - REAL-TIME SYSTEMS
Cloudflare, Inc. | San Francisco, CA (Hybrid)

ABOUT CLOUDFLARE
Cloudflare is on a mission to help build a better Internet. We protect and accelerate any Internet application without adding hardware, installing software, or changing a line of code. Our network handles over 50 million HTTP requests per second at peak, making us one of the largest networks in the world.

ABOUT THE ROLE
We're looking for a Senior Software Engineer to join our Real-Time Systems team. You'll be working on cutting-edge technology that powers real-time communication for millions of users worldwide. This role offers the opportunity to work on distributed systems at massive scale, collaborate with world-class engineers, and make a significant impact on products used by developers globally.

WHAT YOU'LL DO
• Design, build, and maintain highly available real-time communication systems serving millions of concurrent connections
• Architect and implement low-latency data pipelines for audio/video streaming applications
• Lead technical design discussions and mentor junior team members
• Collaborate with product managers to translate business requirements into technical specifications
• Participate in on-call rotation and drive incident response for critical systems
• Contribute to open-source projects and represent Cloudflare at technical conferences
• Write clean, well-tested, and documented code following best practices

WHAT WE'RE LOOKING FOR

Required:
• 5+ years of professional software engineering experience
• Strong proficiency in at least one systems programming language (Go, Rust, C++, or similar)
• Experience building and operating distributed systems at scale
• Deep understanding of networking protocols (TCP/UDP, WebSocket, WebRTC)
• Experience with cloud infrastructure (AWS, GCP, or similar)
• Strong problem-solving skills and ability to debug complex distributed systems
• Excellent communication skills and ability to work in a collaborative environment
• BS/MS in Computer Science or equivalent practical experience

Nice to Have:
• Experience with real-time communication systems (WebRTC, SIP, etc.)
• Familiarity with Cloudflare Workers or edge computing platforms
• Experience with Kubernetes and container orchestration
• Contributions to open-source projects
• Experience leading technical projects or mentoring engineers
• Knowledge of audio/video codecs and streaming protocols

TECH STACK
• Languages: Go, Rust, TypeScript
• Infrastructure: Kubernetes, Docker, Terraform
• Databases: PostgreSQL, ClickHouse, Redis
• Messaging: Kafka, NATS
• Observability: Prometheus, Grafana, Jaeger
